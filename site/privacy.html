SnapTable Reminder
Privacy Support

Effective date: 2026-06-10

Privacy Policy

SnapTable Reminder is designed as a local-only screenshot and document organization utility.

Data You Enter

The app lets you enter or extract record titles, categories, amounts, dates, reminders, notes, phone numbers, email addresses, locations, and recognized OCR text. This information is stored locally on your device.

Image and OCR Processing

When you import an image, the app uses Apple on-device text recognition frameworks to extract text. Version 1 does not send your images, recognized text, or saved records to a server.

Data Collection

Version 1 does not create user accounts, does not use third-party analytics, does not track users, and does not use a backend or cloud AI parser.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, the app schedules local notifications on your device before saved dates.

Export

If you export CSV, you choose where to share or save the file through the iOS share sheet.

Deleting Data

You can delete all local records from the Settings screen in the app.

Advice Disclaimer

SnapTable Reminder helps organize personal records and reminders. It does not provide legal, medical, tax, financial, or investment advice.

Contact

For support or privacy questions, use the support page or open a support request on GitHub.
