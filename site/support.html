SnapTable Reminder Help

SnapTable Reminder helps organize screenshots and document photos into editable records, local reminders, and CSV exports.

Common Questions

Does the app upload my documents?

No. Version 1 processes selected images on device and stores saved records locally.

Can I edit extracted information?

Yes. The app shows parsed fields for review before saving. You can edit titles, dates, amounts, categories, notes, and reminder settings.

Why did OCR miss some text?

Image quality, handwriting, glare, layout, and language can affect recognition. You can paste text or add a record manually when OCR is incomplete.

How do I delete my data?

Open Settings in the app and choose Delete All Local Data.
