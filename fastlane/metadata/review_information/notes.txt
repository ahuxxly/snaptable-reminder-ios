SnapTable Reminder is a local-first productivity utility for turning screenshots and document photos into editable records, local reminders, and CSV exports.

Text recognition runs on device using Apple frameworks. Saved records, recognized OCR text, and reminder settings are stored locally on the user's device. Version 1 does not create accounts, connect to a backend, use third-party analytics, track users, or send document content to a cloud AI parser.

Users can import an image, scan a document, paste text, or add a record manually. Parsed fields are always shown for user review and editing before saving.

The app helps organize personal records and reminders. It does not provide legal, medical, tax, financial, or investment advice.
